Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-free options. Please inform us of any restrictions when making your reservation.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. There's also secure street parking available nearby.

Can I host a private event at Ona's Kitchen?

Yes! We have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 20 guests. For larger events, we can arrange exclusive use of our main dining area. Contact us at events@onaskitchen.co.za for more details.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

We offer takeaway orders for selected menu items. Please call us to place your order. Delivery is available within a 5km radius through our partner services.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. For parties of 8 or more, we require 48-hour notice. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you have a dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While we don't require formal attire, we ask that guests avoid beachwear, flip-flops, and overly casual clothing.
